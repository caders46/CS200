Use Case: Bill Healthcare includes Customer Validation

Context: The actor wants to validate the customer via swipe card

Actors: Provider

Main Success Scenario: 
	1. The actor swipes the customer's card.
	2. The associated customer number is found in the Data Center and recognized as valid.
	3. The process of billing healthcare proceeds.

Extensions:
	2a. The associated customer number is not found in the Data Center or it is found and identified as suspended.
